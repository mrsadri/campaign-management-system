For Sellers:

  1. Open src/seller/en/dashboard.html
  2. Browse active campaigns
  3. Click any campaign to see details and use calculator
  4. Check notifications and earnings history

  1. Open src/admin/en/dashboard.html
  2. View all campaigns (active, draft, completed)
  3. Click "New Campaign" to create with 4-step wizard
  4. Add insurance companies, configure conditions, select participants
